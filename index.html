Job Application Bot
Python · PDF generation · Claude AI
A fully automated system that generates tailored CVs and cover letters in PDF format for each job offer. The bot reads a job description, adapts the content, selects the most relevant experiences, matches the language of the offer (German or English), and outputs production-ready documents — including the CV and cover letter for this very application.
  • Detects offer language automatically and generates all documents in the same language
  • Adapts CV content per offer — experience selection, keywords, profile text
  • Two CV modes: visual two-column layout and ATS-safe single-column format
  • Compresses and embeds photo automatically to keep PDFs under LinkedIn's 2MB limit
  • Driven by a structured JSON data model — clean separation of content and rendering
